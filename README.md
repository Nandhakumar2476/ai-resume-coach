📌 About the Project

Resume AI Coach is an AI-powered career assistant designed to help students and job seekers create professional, ATS-friendly resumes. The platform analyzes resumes, identifies skill gaps, and provides personalized recommendations to improve employability and interview success.

---

🎯 Problem Statement

Many students and fresh graduates struggle to build resumes that meet industry standards and ATS requirements. As a result, they miss job opportunities despite having the necessary skills.

Resume AI Coach solves this problem by providing intelligent resume analysis and career guidance powered by AI.

✅ AI Resume Analysis

✅ ATS Score Evaluation
